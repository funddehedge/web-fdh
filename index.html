01 / approach

"We are hedged first, and directional second."

Fund de Hedgé was built on a simple premise: most funds are sold on their best quarter, and ours is built to survive the worst one. As a newly launched manager, we are not asking allocators to take a track record on faith — we are asking them to judge the construction of the book itself.

Every position is sized against its downside before its upside is ever discussed. We run a concentrated core of high-conviction ideas, hedged with liquid instruments chosen specifically to offset the risks we did not intend to take — because unintended risk, not the market, is what erodes capital over time.
